Shivaji was born in the hill-fort of Shivneri, near the city of Junnar, which is now in Pune district. Scholars disagree on his date of birth. The Government of Maharashtra lists 19 February as a holiday commemorating Shivaji's birth (Shivaji Jayanti).[a][21][22] Shivaji was named after a local deity, the goddess Shivai Devi.[23][24] Shivaji's father Shahaji Bhonsle was a Maratha general who served the Deccan Sultanates.[25] His mother was Jijabai the daughter of Lakhuji Jadhavrao of Sindhkhed, a Mughal-aligned sardar claiming descent from a Yadav royal family of Devagiri
